About FTC Power Play

For my involvement in the First Tech Challenge (FTC) Power Play, I led the development of Java programs for both the autonomous and manual sections of the challenge. These programs played a crucial role in the successful execution of our team's robot during competitions.

The autonomous programs were designed to make the robot perform predefined tasks without direct human control, showcasing precision and efficiency. The manual control programs allowed our team to operate the robot seamlessly during teleoperated periods, contributing to our overall strategy and performance.

The codebase for this project is available on GitHub.

Project Impact

The programs that I have created had a direct impact on our team's success in competitions. Through the development of efficient and reliable Java programs, our robot consistently performed well in both autonomous and manual modes, contributing to our overall achievements.
