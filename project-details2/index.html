About Ion Inc.

Ion Inc. is an educational app created for the 2021 Congressional App Challenge in district NJ-03. The purpose of this app is to teach kids about the various kinds of renewable energy in an entertaining way. We discovered and undertook this project with less than a month before the submittions were due as a way to challenge ourselves. Through interactive and engaging content, Ion Inc. aims to make learning about renewable energy sources a fun and enjoyable experience for young minds.
